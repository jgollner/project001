ABOUT OUR
STUDIO

Founded in 2000, TIP has evolved into a full-service multi-disciplinary design studio with a diverse focus on projects from real estate development to boutique stores and restaurants.



We believe that good ideas come in many forms. Envisioning to fully imagine your new offering. Storytelling to illuminate the audience psyche. Strategic Creative that speaks to your market and differentiates your brand.


We approach every project as a unique opportunity to develop appropriate programs tailored to the specific challenges and opportunities presented by each partnership. And although we work in diverse areas of design, every project, whether large or small is approached with the same values and principles that are the foundation of our company; making great partnerships and enjoying the work that we do. We always aim to work with partners that we not only like, but aspire to learn and grow with and partner with the best in the industry so the job not only gets done, but it gets done right.
